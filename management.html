Board of Director

Hisar H. Siahaan

Chief Executive Officer

Hisar H. Siahaan is the CEO and one of the original founders of LEXCORP. Hisar is not a new name in the RF Cellular industry. He started working with PT SATELINDO in the early era of GSM industry in 1994 as RF Design Planner Coordinator after finishing his Master degree in Electrical and Computer engineering at the University of Kansas. He moved to Nokia Indonesia in 1995 as RF Engineer. Briefly in Nokia, Hisar decided to move to Ericsson Indonesia on 1996. With Ericsson he became the RF manager (1997). At Ericsson Hisar strived to increase the competences of the local engineers with the understanding that solutions to RF issues are tightly related to the local knowledge, geographical local knowledge as well as social local knowledge. Ability to communicate with local operators RF Engineers are also one important key for successful RF Service delivery. During the regional economic problems in 1998 Hisar has managed to provide many trainings for him and his RF Engineer team by working in various projects in Singapore, Taiwan, Turkey, Israel and Spain. Hisar then momentarily left Ericsson Indonesia to work in a new innovative company Little Feet in San Diego ,California, USA in 2000 as System Analyst. Hisar returned to Ericsson Indonesia in 2001 and back to RF service area as Service Delivery Manager for Telkomsel. He changed positions as PROJECT MANAGER and as a part of the account team as technical manager, for Telkomsel Account. He was called back in on a project in in the US (2004) : BSS Changeover of the greater city of Los Angeles from Nokia BSS to Ericsson BSS. In a team of 30 RF engineers from more than 15 different countries, Hisar lead a team of 8 engineer for a Northern area of Los Angeles. Upon the end of the service period, in 2005 Hisar went back to Indonesia and started leading LEXCORP at the beginning of the ARWANA project in Kalimantan. Hisar was known as one of the hands-on capable CEO in LEXCORP
