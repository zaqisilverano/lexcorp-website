Telecom Tower Companies To See 10% Growth Over 2 Years

M2M Communication is dealing with connecting all devices, including household appliances, automobiles, vending machines, industrial machinery, and other equipment, to a network with the intention of making them interchange data and accordingly perfoming actions without human’ s manual interventions. M2M Communication (M2MC) is growing at the average of 12% per year since 2011, and hence M2MC has become one of the most powerful driving forces of the spectacular internet growth. It is estimated that by the year of 2020, there will be about three orders of magnitude more terminals involved in M2M communication than in Human-to-human communication.


Some samples of the M2M technology applications:
1. In the energy harvesting industry, M2M technology is applied to send sensory data of many sensors for temperatures, pressures, flow rates as well as piping gauge status to main operation room for monitoring and adjusting to achieve maximum efficiency as well as for safety controls

2. Streets and highway traffic controls: dynamic environement of the traffic requires continuous monitoring and adjustment to find the best traffic control and maximize traffic flow.

3. Telemedicine, specifically the sensor would monitor and send data of heart transplant. The data is sent to implanted devices that deliver a shock to correct an errant rhythm.
